About Us

Founded in 1977, Climbers Circle has evolved into one of India’s most prominent mountaineering and adventure clubs, with esteemed affiliations to: Indian Mountaineering Foundation and West Bengal Mountaineering and Adventure Sports Foundation (Department of Youth Services, Government of West Bengal), we are dedicated to the art and discipline of climbing, exploration, and environmental responsibility.

Our members have pioneered routes and set new standards in mountaineering across the globe, participating in high-altitude expeditions, technical rock climbing, and adventurous treks through India’s diverse landscapes. We offer rock climbing courses, sustainable outdoor practices, and a supportive community for climbers of all experience levels.

Join us as we push the boundaries of adventure while fostering respect for nature, exploring breathtaking terrain, and inspiring a love for the mountains in every journey.
